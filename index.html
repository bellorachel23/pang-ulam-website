Healthy bitter melon with eggs.

Adobong Manok
  1. Heat oil and sauté garlic.
  2. Add chicken and brown it.
  3. Pour soy sauce and vinegar.
  4. Add water and simmer.
  5. Cook until tender and sauce thickens.
Sinigang na Bangus
  1. Boil water and add tomatoes.
  2. Add bangus and cook.
  3. Put sinigang mix.
  4. Add vegetables.
  5. Simmer until cooked.
Sinigang na Baboy
  1. Boil pork until tender.
  2. Add tomatoes and onion.
  3. Put sinigang mix.
  4. Add vegetables.
  5. Simmer and serve.
Ampalaya with Egg
  1. Sauté garlic and onion.
  2. Add sliced ampalaya.
  3. Cook briefly.
  4. Add beaten eggs.
  5. Stir and serve.
